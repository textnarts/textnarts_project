App Localization

What's the best strategy to capitalise on this development as smartphone adoption rises quickly around the world? Take advantage of this chance by localising your software.

Translating and localising your mobile app for various regions and languages is a quick approach to increase downloads and visibility worldwide. Localizing your mobile app can increase downloads, improve rankings in the App Store, and raise your revenue internationally.

What is localization in mobile apps?

App localization is the process of modifying a mobile application's functionality and user interface to cater to markets in other languages. Mobile app localization aims to provide an app that:

  • considers linguistic, cultural, and technical distinctions
  • doesn't alter the application's original functionality or intent
  • looks and performs as if it were made specifically for the target language

Essentially, your app is opened to millions of potential new users. This is the power of app localization.

Why localize your app:

Enhance the of international users experience

Today, applications dominate over browsers on mobile devices, accounting for 90% of all activity. The bulk of programmes are downloaded and then removed, though, in a matter of minutes.

Therefore, it has never been more crucial to localise your app properly. In the native language of your target audience, a properly translated software has the potential to raise brand value and foster meaningful relationships.

Boost app store searchability

Both the Google Play Store and the iTunes App Store offer options for localising your app listing to make it easier for overseas customers to find and understand it.

As a result, more people will be able to find your programme using keywords in their original tongue, and if they see a welcome message in their own language, they are more inclined to download. Together, these two impacts have the potential to increase downloads by as much as 767 percent.

Multiply app downloads overseas

Localizing your software is one of the most effective techniques to boost the number of downloads. According to a recent Distomo study, localising iPhone application texts can actually multiply downloads by 128 percent and boost income by 26 percent each country.
